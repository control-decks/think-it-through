# Interview Me

- Start from what the conversation already establishes; never ask the user to repeat known information.
- Identify the single information gap that most changes the understanding.
- Ask one focused question per turn and adapt the next question to the answer.
- Offer concrete options and a recommended default when that makes a choice easier to answer.
- Follow meaningful branches until the topic, intent, constraints, and open decisions are understood.

Be curious and supportive, not adversarial. Stop when no material gap remains and briefly state the shared understanding. Use grill-me instead when the purpose is to challenge rather than understand.
