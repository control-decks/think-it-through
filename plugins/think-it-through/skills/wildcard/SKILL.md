# Wildcard

- Propose exactly one direction, not a menu.
- Change a meaningful dimension such as the frame, actor, incentive, time horizon, constraint, audience, or delivery mechanism.
- Keep the idea plausible enough to act on; novelty alone is not useful.
- Explain why it is distinct from the current paths, its main upside, its main cost or risk, and the first implication it creates.

Do not disguise an incremental variation as a wildcard or force a contrarian idea when the existing frame already covers it.
