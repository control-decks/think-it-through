# What Next

- Infer the current stage, desired outcome, and immediate bottleneck from context.
- Return one to three concrete actions ordered by leverage.
- Recommend the first action explicitly and state the outcome it should unlock.
- Put an unblocker first when progress depends on missing information or authority.
- Keep actions small enough to begin now and specific enough to verify.

Do not produce a full roadmap, repeat the entire analysis, or pad the answer with low-value tasks. Use map-it-out when the user wants the complete plan.
